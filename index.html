How to get started with Magnetocoin

1. Pick a Wallet

Pick a Wallet from our Wallet Section
A Wallet is used for holding your Magnetocoins directly on your Computer/Smartphone.

2. Configure your Wallet

After downloading, you can configure your Wallet according to our Guide

3. Get some Magnetocoins

There are numerous amounts of ways to get Magnetocoin. You can buy them, trade for them, get tipped, "mine" them, and more. They are surprisingly endless!

You can get tipped Magnetocoin by participating in our community
If that's not your style, another way to have your first Magnetocoin is to participate at Folding@Home.

Folding@Home (FaH) is a distributed computing project. You can join team mgtfolders, bring in your computing power and receive some mgts for your efforts.

You can also get Magnetocoin by "mining" it. Mining is when you use your computer to process Magnetocoin transactions by other people, and in return you get Magnetocoin.
Mining is intended for advanced users only, as it requires a lot of technical knowledge to do. If you are interested, you can start mining today by using the command setgenerate true in your wallet's console.
